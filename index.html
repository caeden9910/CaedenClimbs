Head in the clouds Life hundreds of feet up

From Utah to California, Washington to Nevada, every climb offers a unique mental and physical challenge. Climbing is unlike anything else, a test of yourself on some of the most stunning landscapes mother nature has to offer. I climb not to prove anything but because when I am hundreds of feet above the ground, risking it all, when I am closest to losing it all, thats when I feel closest to it all.

Trad to Sport, Bouldering to Alpine Follow the journey

My goal is to climb the most beautiful, the most stunning, and the hardest routes in America starting right here in my home of Utah.
