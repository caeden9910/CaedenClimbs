Who I am

Welcome to the adventure where I climb cool stuff! I am a Brigham Young University student addicted to the ascent. I live and climb predominately in Utah. A growth mindset is my focus and everyday I try to send harder, be happier, and adventure farther.

How I started climbing

So there I am, fresh into my first semester of college. I didn't know who I was, what I liked, or what I wanted to do. In this exploration into who I was, I happened to meet this girl who I quickly got feelings for. One day she invited me to the climbing gym and out of sheer male testosterone I decided to hop one of the hardest routes in the gym, I barely made it up the route and it completely wore me out to the point that the rest of the time we were at the climbing gym, I hated it. Upon completing the session this girl invited me to buy a climbing gym pass, I was still enthralled with her and thus I obliged. A week later I lost feels and stopped talking to the girl...but I still had this gym pass, the rest is history.
